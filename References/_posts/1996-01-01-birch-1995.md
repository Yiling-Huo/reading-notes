Linguistic analyses of the relation between focus and prosody note that it is possible for certain accented constituents within pitch accent a broadly focused phrase to project focus to the entire phrase. We manipulated focus requirements and accent in recorded question-answer question-answer pairs and asked listeners to make linguistic judgments of prosodic dialogs appropriateness (Experiments 1 and 3) or to make judgments based on meaningful comprehension (Experiments 2 and 4).
